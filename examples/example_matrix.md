Spaces & line breaks are ignored, used for formatting.

# Example 1 
(filenames, no formatting)  
[001      | 003 | 004]
[009 | 1723 | 866]
[109 |006|543]
  
# Example 2 
(filename, variant formatting)  
[001      | 003 | 004]  
[009 | 1723 | 866]  
[109 |006|543]  

# Example 3
(exact hex string)  
[0x | 0x | 0x]  
[0x | 0x | 0x]  
[0x | 0x | 0x]
